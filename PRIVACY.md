# Privacy Policy — YouTube Chatbot Chrome Extension

Last updated: May 2026

## Data We Collect
This extension does not collect any personally identifiable information.

## What We Send to Our Server
When you ask a question, the extension sends:
- The YouTube video URL (this is public information)
- Your typed question

This data is used only to fetch the video transcript and generate 
an answer. It is not stored, logged, or shared with any third party.

## Third Party Services
We use SerpAPI to fetch YouTube transcripts and Groq AI to generate 
answers. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.
